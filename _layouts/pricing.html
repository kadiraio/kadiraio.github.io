Common Questions

What is the Solo Plan?

Solo plan is a special plan targetted toward indi-developers with a limited budget.

It has most of our paid features. But, it can't have collobarators and have a limit of 5 alerts per an app.

What is a DDP Session?

A DDP Session is realtime connection made to your Meteor server. It can be made either from a browser or from a mobile app.

What is Data Retention?

Data Retention indicates the period of time we keep your application data with us.

Can I change plans?

Of course you can change plans anytime.

What happens when I increase DDP Sessions limit

We don't automatically block your app. We'll inform you before you reach limits or after you've exceeded. Then if you don't need to upgrade, you will be transferred to a forever free with a limited set of features.

How many hosts/servers/vms an app can have?

There are no hard limitations. But we'll monitor for abuse.

Tell me more about Collaborators?

You can simply allow other members in your team to use Kadira UI to monitor and optimize your app. You can change the ownership to another user as well.

What are Alerts?

Kadira Alerts sends you a notification when something strange happens with your app. You can set alerts for almost every metric available in Kadira.

What is Error Tracking?

Kadira tracks both server and client errors for you automatically. You can use Kadira UI to analyze error traces and understand them.
